How to Wear Alone

Safely Wearing Alone

1 Spread the carrier on a flat sofa or bed.
2 Unfasten the shoulder buckle and side buckle.
3 Lay your baby on the carrying part.
4 Fasten the shoulder buckle and side buckle again.
5 Sit your baby on a sofa or bed with a backrest.
6 Put your arms through the vest carrier like putting on a backpack and zip up.
üí° Tips for Wearing Alone
It's safer to do this in a place with a backrest. Start slowly at first, and you can do it faster once you get used to it.

Precautions

This warning is based on Korean and European safety standards for infant carriers.
Please check before use.

‚ö†Ô∏è Fall Warning
Please check the following to prevent fall accidents:

‚Ä¢ Small babies can slip through leg openings.
‚Ä¢ Always check that the baby's legs are spread and the baby is in the center of the seat.
‚Ä¢ The user's standing balance affects both the user and the baby's movement.
‚Ä¢ Always be careful when bending or leaning.
‚Ä¢ Always check buckles/zippers/snap buttons/straps/adjustment devices before use.
‚Ä¢ Check the product condition for any abnormalities every time you use it.
‚Ä¢ The user's hands should always be in a position to protect the baby in case of unexpected dangerous situations.

‚ö†Ô∏è Usage Precautions

  • This carrier is designed only for babies weighing 3.5kg ~ 15kg.
  • This carrier was developed for daily use purposes, so never use it during active exercise.
  • When you are not familiar with the usage, use it on a cushioned floor or with the help of an assistant.
  • Always practice the wearing method in front of a mirror.
  • Always check buckles/zippers/snap buttons/straps/adjustment devices before use.
  • Do not use electrical appliances or cook while using the carrier, and do not consume hot drinks/food.
  • After first using this carrier, always check if your baby dislikes it.

Proper Washing Method

üß∫

Proper washing method for long-lasting use of the product

‚úì Washing Method

  • Do not use bleach.
  • Hand wash in cold water with neutral detergent.
  • Always wash separately.
  • Do not soak for long periods, and do not wash with hot water or boil.
  • Never machine dry.
  • After washing, lay flat and dry in a well-ventilated shade away from direct sunlight.
  • When using a washing machine, use wool cycle and always put in a laundry bag for separate washing.
  • For minor stains, pat with a wet towel or spot wash, and avoid frequent machine washing.
  • Even baby-specific detergents may contain alkaline ingredients that can cause discoloration and fading, so use neutral detergent.
  • Drying in a drum washer or dryer can cause product shrinkage, damage, and deformation, so dry naturally.
‚ö†Ô∏è Important
Before washing, please check the precautions.
Issues caused by incorrect washing and drying are not grounds for exchange or return.
