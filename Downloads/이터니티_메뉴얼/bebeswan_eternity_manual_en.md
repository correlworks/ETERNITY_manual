## 01. Proper Wearing Method
*Page 5*

### Key Instructions:

1. **Waist Belt Position**
   - Wear the waist belt above your pelvis and tighten it as much as possible.

2. **Shoulder Strap Adjustment**
   - Adjust the shoulder straps according to your baby's growth.

3. **Side Strap Adjustment**
   - Tighten the side straps for better weight distribution, making it more comfortable for your shoulders and waist.

4. **Leg Buckle (Outward Facing)**
   - Release the leg buckle when carrying baby facing outward.

> ğŸ’¡ **Important TIP**
> - **Facing inward:** Adjust the (Lumi-Lock) buckle according to baby's growth.
> - **Facing outward:** Release the (Lumi-Lock) leg buckle.
> - **Tightening all straps** distributes weight evenly for comfortable extended wear.

## 02. Checklist When Carrying Baby
*Page 6*

### Check These 3 Things!

1. **Hold the zipper with one hand and the top of the zipper with the other hand** to align them properly.

2. **Leave about a hand span from the bottom of the zipper** - it will go up easily.

3. **Double-Lock Zipper** - Auto-locks when pulled up, press again for double lock!

## 06. Booster Usage Method
*Pages 10-11*

### Using as a Chair Booster

**Step 1:**
- Put the carrier on the chair backrest
- Seat baby facing outward

**Step 2:**
- Tie the auxiliary belt securely around the seating area and seat panel together

**Step 3:**
- Pass the waist belt and side belt under the seat panel through the chair legs and fasten
- Cross the side belt while wrapping around baby's waist and fasten

**Step 4:**
- Tighten the shoulder belt all the way and wrap the two connecting straps once, then fasten with snap button

**Step 5:**
- Release the leg buckle

**Step 6:**
- Tighten all adjustment straps

> ğŸ’¡ **Booster Usage Tip**
> While using as a booster, parents should always watch the baby and never leave unattended.

## 07. Newborn Usage Method
*Page 12*

### Safe Newborn Wearing Method

1. Put on the carrier and **lean back on a sofa**.

2. Release the shoulder buckle.

3. **Loosen** the side straps.

4. Hold the baby while **supporting the hips and neck well**.

5. Re-fasten the shoulder buckle and **tighten the side buckle**.

> âš ï¸ **Important**
> - **Fetal position:** Keep leg buckle fastened.
> - **Normal position:** Release the leg buckle.

## 08. How to Wear Alone
*Page 13*

### Wearing Safely by Yourself

1. **Spread the carrier on a flat sofa or bed.**

2. Release the shoulder buckle and side buckle.

3. Lay baby on the carrying section.

4. **Re-fasten the shoulder buckle and side buckle.**

5. **Seat baby on a sofa or bed with back support.**

6. **Put your arms through the vest carrier like a backpack** and zip up.

> ğŸ’¡ **Solo Wearing Tip**
> It's safer to do this somewhere with back support. Start slowly at first, and you'll get faster with practice.

## 10. Precautions
*Page 15*

> This warning is based on Korean and European safety standards for baby carriers.
> Please check before use.

### âš ï¸ Fall Warning

**Please check the following to prevent fall accidents:**

- Small babies may slip through the leg openings.
- Always ensure baby's legs are spread and centered on the seat.
- The user's standing balance is affected by the movement of both user and baby.
- Be extremely careful when bending or leaning.
- Always check buckles/zippers/snap buttons/straps/adjustments before use.
- Check product condition each time before use.
- User's hands should always be ready to protect baby in case of emergency.

### âš ï¸ Usage Precautions

- This carrier is designed only for babies weighing 3.5kg ~ 15kg.
- This carrier is designed for daily use, never use during active exercise.
- When unfamiliar with usage, use on cushioned surfaces or with assistance.
- Practice wearing method in front of a mirror.
- Always check buckles/zippers/snap buttons/straps/adjustments before use.
- Do not use electrical appliances or cook while using the carrier, and do not eat hot beverages/food.
- After first use, check if baby dislikes the carrier.

## 11. Proper Washing Method
*Page 16*

### ğŸ§º Washing Instructions

- Do not use bleach.
- Hand wash with cold water and neutral detergent.
- Always wash separately.
- Do not soak for long periods, and do not wash with hot water or boil.
- Never machine dry.
- After washing, lay flat and dry in a well-ventilated shaded area away from direct sunlight.
- When using washing machine, use wool cycle with laundry net and wash separately.
- For minor stains, dab with wet towel or spot clean. Avoid frequent machine washing.
- Even baby-specific detergents may contain alkaline components that can cause discoloration, so use neutral detergent.
- Drum dryers and machine drying can cause shrinkage and product damage, so air dry naturally.

> âš ï¸ **Important**
> Please check precautions before washing.
> Issues caused by improper washing and drying are not grounds for exchange or return.
